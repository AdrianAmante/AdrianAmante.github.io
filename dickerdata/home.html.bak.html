The Endpoint Security Market is predicted to be worth $17.38 Billion USD by 2020*

Endpoint security solutions secure endpoints and remote devices used in organisations from viruses, trojans, malwares as well as advanced threats, such as zero-day malwares and advanced persistent threats. As the frequency of sophisticated endpoint attacks on enterprises is growing, the market is expected to gain traction in the next five years, representing significant growth and opportunity for IT resellers.

Major Market Forces

The major forces driving the endpoint security market are the need to mitigate IT security risks, growing Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) trends among organisations and an increase in the frequency of endpoint attacks. New and sophisticated threats such as zero-day malwares, trojans, and advanced persistent threats are created everyday putting your customers critical company data at risk.

These factors have encouraged organisations to deploy endpoint security solution to safeguard their endpoints and networks against potential cyber-attacks.

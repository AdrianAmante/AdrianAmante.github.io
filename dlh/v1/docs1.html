Opportunity created

Opportunities are created after you customer completes an inquiry form or because they are one of you top engaged subscribers

Pass to tele-agency

As an optional you may ask for a 3rd partner tele-marketing agency to call on your behalf. This activity is funded by HPE to help you further qualify your opportunities and drive sales.

Agency calls on your behalf

The tele-agency "Raw Marketing", based in Sydney, will call your customer on your behalf and attempt to uncover as much information about their upcoming projects.

Agency provide outcome

Once RAW have complete their calls, they'll update the opportunity with an outcome and provide details notes of the call. You'll be sent a real-time notification once this is complete.

You follow up customer

Now that you have detailed insights into your customers requirement, it's over to you to follow up with them.

You complete the sale

Just as you'd expect, you continue to quote and sell the solution as per normal.

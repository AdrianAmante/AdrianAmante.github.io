How it Works

You approve your e-newsletter to be send to you customers and prospects

Your customers receive the email opening and clicking to read more

When a customer completes a form or requesting more information, you're sent a real-time notification

You can now access this 'opportunity' via OneAffiniti's Lead Hub

You can keep track of your customer inquiries and update them with notes and close when you win

As an optional process, you may ask for a 3rd partner tele-marketing agency to call on your behalf

Once you confirm to "pass to agency", the telemarketing team will make 3 call attempts to connect to the customer and attempt to uncover more information about the opportunity and to help secure you a meeting

During this time you will be unable to edit this opportunity until the telemarketing team have completed their calls

Upon call completion you’ll be sent an update notification and you will then be able to continue editing

1. Approve your e-newsletter 2. Customers receive email 3. Real time notification 4. Access via Lead Hub 5. Keep track 6. 3rd partner agency 7. Connect to the customer 8. Telemarketing team 9. Sent notification

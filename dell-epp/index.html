The Dell EPP is available to employees of valued client organisations. To access the Dell EPP offers, you will need to sign up with your work (not personal) email address.

Every month you will receive an email with Dell EPP promotional codes which can be used to access employee discounts in addition to any other promotional price being offered by Dell at that time. These exclusive Dell EPP offers are available across a wide range of Dell products, including laptops, desktops, printers and much more.
